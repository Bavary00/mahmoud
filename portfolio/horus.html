Eye Of Horus

We built a dynamic web app which is a simulation of the globe and every satellite that's out there with all of the information about them concluding (Name, country, ID, perigee, apogee, size....etc.) and the satellite's orbit around the globe, all of that is displayed to the user when requested (clicking on the satellite).

" The website achieved a great performance in Nasa Space Apps Cairo 2020 competition, As the website got the first place in its category and nominated for the global competition. "
